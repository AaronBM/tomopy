circular_roi¶

tomopy.algorithms.preprocess.circular_roi.circular_roi(data, ratio)[source]¶

Apply circular mask to projection data.

Parameters:

data : ndarray, float32

3-D reconstructed data with dimensions: [projections, slices, pixels]

ratio : scalar, int

Ratio of the circular mask’s diameter in pixels to the number of reconstructed image pixels (i.e., the dimension of the images).

output : ndarray
